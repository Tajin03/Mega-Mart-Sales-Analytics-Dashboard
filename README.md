### **💡 Core Metrics Overview**
At first glance, the numbers look strong:
* 💰 **$118.73M** in Gross Revenue
* 📈 **$16.89M** in Net Profit
* 📊 **14.23%** Actual Profit Margin

But the real value came from digging into the **“why”** behind our geographic and promotional footprint.

---

### **𝗞𝗲𝘆 𝗜𝗻𝘀𝗶𝗴𝗵𝘁𝘀:**

* **The Bubble Size Deception (Geospatial Mapping):** Large revenue clusters on the map don't always equate to high profit margins. A few high-volume countries generate massive top-line numbers but suffer severe margin compression due to localized heavy discounting.
* **Hidden Profit Hotspots:** Certain regional store locations exhibit smaller revenue footprints but carry significantly higher profit density. These areas represent our most efficient operational models.
* **The Discount Dilemma:** High discounts don’t inherently scale gains. They show diminishing returns, exposing where aggressive price-cutting triggers severe revenue leakage rather than real customer lifetime value.

---

### **𝗧𝗲𝗰𝗵𝗻𝗶𝗰𝗮𝗹 𝗙𝗼𝗼𝘁𝗽𝗿𝗶𝗻𝘁:**

This project moves past static reports to deliver an interactive, executive-ready analytical experience. To achieve this, I implemented:
* **Advanced DAX Modeling:** Developed complex cross-table measures to track discounted metrics, net margins, and revenue leakage across multiple transaction boundaries.
* **Geographic Data Categorization:** Configured geospatial model properties (`City`, `State/Province`, `Country`) to map retail concentrations across geographical boundaries accurately.
* **Interactive Architecture:** Layered advanced visual filters (demographics, category segments, and time intelligence) for a fully cross-filtered dashboard layout.
