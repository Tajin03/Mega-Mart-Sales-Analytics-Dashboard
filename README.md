This **Sales & Profit Performance Dashboard** built in Power BI provides a deep-dive analysis into the retail operations of Mega Mart. By cross-examining top-line revenue against actual net yields, product subcategories, promotional markdowns, and consumer demographics, this dashboard uncovers where value is being generated and where it is leaking.

### **💡 Core High-Level Operational Metrics**
The high-level KPIs establish a robust baseline for Mega Mart's retail footprint:
* **Total Product Cost:** \$120.511M
* **Gross Revenue:** \$177.938M
* **Total Net Profit:** \$57M
* **Total Transactions:** 1K (1,000 orders processed)
* **Total Customers:** 200 active buyers
* **Total Products:** 50 distinct SKUs

### **𝗞𝗲𝘆 𝗜𝗻𝘀𝗶𝗴𝗵𝘁𝘀 & Data Storytelling:**

* **Categorical Dominance:** The **Electronics** segment acts as the primary powerhouse for the business, driving over half of all top-line revenue (**53.61%** or **\$37M**), closely followed by **Fashion** at **41.61%** (**\$29M**). **Groceries** represent a minor niche at **4.76%** (**\$3M**).
* **Subcategorical Profit Density:** While **Smartphones** drive the absolute highest net returns by a massive margin (~\$0.79M), foundational categories like **Accessories** and **Clothing** provide critical volume-driven baseline profits. 
* **Revenue Leakage via Discounts:** Aggressive promotional strategies have visible consequences. The dashboard exposes a total revenue loss of **\$0.290M** purely to markdown absorption. The **Electronics** division suffered the largest hit (**\$133.97K** in leakages), followed closely by **Fashion** (**\$126.24K**). 
* **Gender Demographics Consistency:** Interestingly, purchase behavior between genders remains exceptionally balanced after discounts are applied. **Male (M)** shoppers contributed marginally higher gross revenue (~99M) compared to **Female (F)** shoppers (~79M), but both tracks maintain identical, stable conversion efficiency post-markdown.
* **Underperforming Stock Optimization:** A targeted analysis of the lowest-performing products reveals that items like **"Understand Camera"** and **"House Accessories"** sit heavily at the bottom of net margins, highlighting immediate candidates for inventory restructuring or stock clearance.

### **𝗧𝗲𝗰𝗵𝗻𝗶𝗰𝗮𝗹 𝗙𝗼𝗼𝘁𝗽𝗿𝗶𝗻𝘁:**

This project utilizes modern dashboarding conventions to translate raw transactional rows into structured business intelligence:
* **Advanced Data Modeling:** Designed an efficient relational schema connecting transactional records, product dimensions, and customer demographics.
* **DAX Measure Engineering:** Formatted complex custom measures to evaluate pre-discount revenue, post-discount yields (`Revenue Generated After Disct`), margin tracking, and isolated revenue leakages.
* **Modern UI/UX Design:** Implemented a dark-mode theme utilizing high-contrast blue visual accents, structured grid alignments, intuitive card matrices, and contextual subtitles for self-service business user exploration.
